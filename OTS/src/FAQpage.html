FAQ'S

How do I determine my correct measurements for ordering a tailored garment online?

We provide a detailed measurement guide on our website, which includes step-by-step instructions and video tutorials. You can easily measure yourself at home using a measuring tape and following our guidelines to ensure the perfect fit.

Can I choose the fabric for my tailored garment?

Absolutely! We offer a wide range of high-quality fabrics for you to choose from. You can browse through our fabric swatches on our website and select the one that suits your preferences and needs.

What is the typical turnaround time for receiving my tailored garment?

Our standard turnaround time is 1.5 weeks from the date of order confirmation. However, please note that this is an estimate, and it may vary depending on the complexity of the garment and current order volume. We strive to deliver your custom-made garment as quickly as possible without compromising on quality.

Can I request alterations or adjustments to my garment after receiving it?

We understand that minor alterations might be needed occasionally. If you require any adjustments, please contact our customer support within 7 days of receiving your order. We will guide you through the process and provide options for alterations, either locally or through our tailoring service.

Can I customize the design of my tailored garment?

Yes, we offer customization options to personalize your garment. You can choose details such as collar style, sleeve length, buttons, pockets, and more. We have a customization interface on our website where you can select your desired options and preview the design before placing your order.

Do you provide international shipping for your tailored garments?

Yes, we offer international shipping to various countries. During the checkout process, you can select your country, and the shipping options and costs will be calculated accordingly. Please note that international shipping times may vary depending on your location and customs procedures in your country.
